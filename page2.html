Golf Equipment & Beginner Tips

Getting Started with the Right Gear

Essential Golf Equipment for Beginners

As a beginner, you don't need 14 clubs right away. Start simple and build up.

Key items include clubs, balls, tees, and comfortable shoes.

A basic set often includes a driver, irons, wedges, and a putter.

Common Golf Clubs and Their Uses

  • Driver - For long tee shots
  • Fairway woods/Hybrids - Versatile for distance
  • Irons - For approach shots to the green
  • Wedges - For short shots and bunkers
  • Putter - For rolling the ball on the green

Quick Steps to Start Playing Golf

  1. Get a beginner club set or borrow some
  2. Visit a driving range to practice
  3. Take a lesson from a pro
  4. Play a par-3 course first
  5. Have fun and be patient!
